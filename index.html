About Me

The Human Behind the Code

I'm a frontend engineer with 6 years of experience building products that live at the intersection of engineering precision and design craft. I believe the web is the world's most powerful creative medium — and I treat it like one.

My specialty is Svelte and SvelteKit — I love its reactivity model and how it gets out of my way. I've shipped production apps serving millions of users, contributed to open source, and obsessed over every millisecond of render time.

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring generative art, 3D graphics, and finding ways to push the browser further than it was designed to go.
